Conflicted natural reserve

USHAKOVSKOYE

Ushakovskoye (Russian: Ушаковское)

Foundation and abandon: [1890s ; 1990s]

Location: Artic ocean, Wrangel island Coordinate: 70°58′51″N 178°29′27″W

Basic infos

The Wrangel island was reached in the 1890s by Georgy Ushakov, the island was habited by Chukchi with no permanant sttlement. Then in 1911, Russia claimed the island, however many other countries were interested in the island, as it was strategic and useful for scientific research and to assess sovregnity in the artic. In 1921, Britain and Canada claimed the island even if it already had been claimed by the Russia, the USSR at that time. When the British and Canadians got onto the island and installed themselves, the USSR responded by sending a gun boat and kicked out the british and canadians, and in 1926, the world recognised Wrangel island as soviet. The condition on Wrangel are very cold and humid (-56°c in december and never more than 19°c recorded, humidity somtimes reaching above 90%), and on top of that, sometimes no sunshine hours for months. After the 1920s, the island was used for research by meteorologist, geologist, biologist, and glaciologist. The island was also used as a waypoint for military operations and surveillance during the cold war The population would fluctuate from around 10 to 200 people, then the island was abandonned at the collapse of the USSR and now the island is a natural reserve.
